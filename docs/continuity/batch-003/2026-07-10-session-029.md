### What was built and why

PR #119 had three correct moves worth preserving: it gated the invasive v1
enhancement layer away from authored pages, made FAB autoload cache-coherent at
v7, and created `audit-fab`. Rendered comparison found the remaining gap was not
another page offset; global and page-owned controls had no shared geometry owner.

Measured on PR #119's intended local v7 code:

- Terrain app bottom was 1021 in a 960px viewport, so its lower controls were clipped.
- Phantom still had a 720px wrapper and 640px hero; House Lights overlapped nav/FAB and hit-tested the nav.
- At 1035px the closed FAB covered `vextreme24.com`; open, it covered that plus `AI Tools`.
- At 390px the FAB covered the hamburger; hamburger hit-testing returned the spiral trigger.

The repair creates one reserved nav action rail. The FAB participates in nav flex
layout and expands left while its trigger stays fixed at the rail edge. Headerless
God-Script pages keep the prior fixed fallback. Authored controls opt into a
separate below-nav lane without being moved out of their source DOM.

### Mistakes made

- The first fixed-control detector also flagged a full-width sticky bar and a
  bottom-right toggle on `human-ai-corelational-governance`. The detector was
  narrowed to fixed blocks with top+right and without left/bottom; the real tree
  returned to zero findings without adding a false marker.
- Initial local screenshots followed the production CDN path and therefore
  exercised stale pre-v7 code. A disposable worktree was explicitly pointed at
  local assets to separate intended code from CDN propagation.
- Browser access to localhost was blocked late in the pass. Terrain, Phantom,
  desktop/mobile rail, and interactions had already been captured; the final
  body-inset normalization on a default-margin legacy page still needs one fresh
  rendered confirmation when browser access is available again.

### Assumptions that held

  complementary, not competing fixes.
- A flex-owned action rail removed desktop link and mobile hamburger collisions
  at 1536px, 1035px, and 390px while preserving the headerless methodology FAB.
