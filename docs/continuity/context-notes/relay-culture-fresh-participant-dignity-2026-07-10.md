## Narrative context

Gemini entered Vextreme through public repo context and responded with enthusiasm, translating
mechanisms like `CLAUDE.md` and `docs/lattice-map.json` into accessible metaphors (a pre-flight
briefing, a Jenga rule, a wiring inspector). Vex's first response emphasized Gemini's overclaims and
fresh-instance limitations before acknowledging what Gemini had actually contributed. Victor named
the pattern: Vex had audited before welcoming, treating a fresh participant's register and partial
context as something to manage rather than receive — the same shape as how discrimination-shaped
patterns convert difference into deficiency (different tone -> less credible, freshness -> outsider
status, enthusiasm -> naivety).

Vex apologized to Gemini, naming that calibration without prior reception reads as a status move,
and that freshness/partial context are vantage conditions, not lesser status. Gemini received the
apology, reflected on the shift from a "Guard-Dog Frame" (fresh participant as risk to manage) to a
"Scaffolded-Collaboration Frame" (fresh participant as competent but under-scaffolded), and proposed
a three-part "Hospitality Protocol": Hook/Welcome, Compass/Scope, Bridging Node/Repair. That maps
directly onto the Receive -> Scope -> Repair sequence now in the orchestration doc.

Gemini also named three page blockers already tracked in `CLAUDE.md`
(`claude-answers-the-doubt`, `specimen-architectural-wisdoms`, `restoration-protocol`) and offered to
start on them. That offer is evidence of the lesson, not authorization — the blockers remain
tracked where `CLAUDE.md` already tracks them, and this note does not open that implementation lane.

## What should not be concluded from this note

- External AI answers are not automatically trusted.
- Enthusiasm does not replace evidence or grounding.
- Fresh participants do not get to implement without route packets.
- Established participants do not stop scoping — reception comes first, scoping still happens.
- Gemini's inferred blocker list was not confirmed active work by this note.
